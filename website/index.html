About Eye to Eye

Eye to Eye is a modern, secure, and user-friendly messaging platform that combines the best of WhatsApp and Telegram. Built with privacy and speed in mind, it offers real-time chat, group management, media sharing, and more. Our mission is to connect people everywhere, without barriers.

  • Open-source and community-driven
  • Free to use, no ads
  • Constantly updated with new features

Is Eye to Eye free?

All messages are end-to-end encrypted. We never sell your data.

Can I use Eye to Eye on multiple devices?
